# 01 Research Documents Schema

Normalized research article entities derived from `00_raw/xml/articles/pmc_oa/`
and `00_raw/json/articles/pmc_oa/`.

## Required fields

- `document_id`
- `document_type` = `research_document`
- `source_system`
- `source_identifiers.pmcid`
- `title`
- `journal`
- `publication_date`
- `license`
- `abstract`
- `primary_entities`
- `raw_sources`
- `provenance`
- `privacy_posture`
